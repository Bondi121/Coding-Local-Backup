Travel Tips

As someone who has explored Colombia extensively, I have some travel tips and recommendations to share:

  • Try the coffee! Colombia produces some of the best coffee in the world.
  • Learn some salsa! Colombian music and dance is a big part of the culture.
  • Take a walking tour of the historic center in Bogotá, it's full of amazing street art and history.
  • Visit Medellín, a vibrant city with great nightlife and beautiful parks.
  • Explore the stunning landscapes of the coffee region and Tayrona National Park.
