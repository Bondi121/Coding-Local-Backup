Colombian Stats

My portfolio as an aspring Data Analyst to display Colombian stats.

About Me

This is my portfolio, showcasing my experience and skills as an aspiring data analyst. It also serves as a platform for me to share my travel experiences in Colombia and provide visualizations of Colombian statistics.

Projects

My project focuses on displaying Colombian statistics using the powerful data visualization library, Matplotlib. Through this project, I aim to provide comprehensive and insightful visualizations of Colombian data, such as population demographics, economic indicators, and social trends. Matplotlib allows me to create dynamic and customizable graphs and charts that help to highlight patterns and relationships in the data. By presenting this information in an accessible and engaging way, I hope to promote a deeper understanding of the country's complex and diverse landscape. Whether you are a student, researcher, or simply interested in learning more about Colombia, my project offers a valuable resource for exploring and interpreting Colombian statistics.
